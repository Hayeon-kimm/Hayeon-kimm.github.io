About

Hi! I'm Hayeon Kim, a Master's student in Artificial Intelligence at UNIST (Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology), affiliated with the LAIT Lab under Professor Jaejun Yoo.

My research sits at the intersection of multimodal AI and generative model evaluation. I'm currently working on a multimodal benchmark for evaluating generative models across multiple modalities.

I've had the privilege of interning at NAVER CLOVA (face landmark & detection), collaborating with Kijiji Canada (LLM-as-a-judge quality scoring), and conducting a research visit at the University of Toronto.
